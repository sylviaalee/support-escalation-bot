## Downgrading
Downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep current limits until then.

## Annual Billing
Save 20% with annual billing. Switch at **Settings > Billing > Billing Cycle**.

## Enterprise Plans Include
- Custom rate limits
- Dedicated support
- SLA guarantees
- SSO/SAML
- Custom data retention policies
- On-premises deployment option

## Cancellation
Cancel anytime at **Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription**. You retain access until period end.
