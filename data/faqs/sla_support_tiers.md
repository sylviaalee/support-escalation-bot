# SLA and Support Tiers

## Support Tiers by Plan

| Plan | Channel | Response Time |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Free | Community forum only | Best effort |
| Pro | Email | 2 business days |
| Business | Email + Chat | 8 business hours |
| Enterprise | Email + Chat + Phone | 1 hour (critical), 4 hours (high) |

## Severity Definitions
- **Critical (P1)**: Production system down, data loss risk
- **High (P2)**: Major feature broken, significant user impact
- **Medium (P3)**: Feature degraded, workaround available
- **Low (P4)**: Minor issue, cosmetic, feature request

## Uptime SLA
- Free/Pro: No SLA
- Business: 99.9% monthly uptime
- Enterprise: 99.99% monthly uptime with credits for violations

## Filing an Urgent Issue
Enterprise customers can call the dedicated support line or use the **Mark as Critical** flag when submitting tickets.

## Status Page
Check **status.example.com** before filing a ticket. Active incidents are posted there in real time.
