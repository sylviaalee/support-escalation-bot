# Account Deletion and Data Export

## Exporting Your Data
Before deleting, export your data:
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy > Export Data**
2. Select data types (projects, files, API logs, billing history)
3. Export is prepared within 24 hours and emailed as a ZIP archive

## Deleting Your Account
1. Navigate to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**
2. Confirm by typing your email address
3. Account is scheduled for deletion in 14 days (grace period)
4. You can cancel deletion within the grace period

## What Gets Deleted
- All projects and associated files
- API keys and integrations
- Personal information
- Usage history

## What Is Retained
- Invoices and billing records (legal requirement, 7 years)
- Anonymized aggregate usage data

## Team Accounts
If you're a team owner, you must transfer ownership or delete the team before deleting your personal account.
