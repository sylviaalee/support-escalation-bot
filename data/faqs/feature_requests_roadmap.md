# Feature Requests and Product Roadmap

## Submitting Feature Requests
1. Visit our public roadmap at **roadmap.example.com**
2. Search to see if it's already requested
3. Click **+ Request Feature** and describe your use case
4. Other users can upvote requests

## How We Prioritize
We evaluate feature requests based on:
- Number of customer requests and upvotes
- Strategic alignment with product direction
- Implementation complexity
- Revenue impact

## Roadmap Categories
- **Shipping Now**: In active development
- **Up Next**: Planned for next 1-2 quarters
- **Exploring**: Under consideration
- **Not Planned**: Won't implement (with explanation)

## Following a Feature
Click **Follow** on any roadmap item to get email updates when its status changes.

## Enterprise Feature Requests
Enterprise customers can escalate feature requests through their Customer Success Manager. High-impact enterprise requests may be fast-tracked or offered as custom development.

## Beta Program
Join our beta program at **beta.example.com** to test upcoming features and provide early feedback.
