# Team and User Management

## Inviting Team Members
1. Go to **Settings > Team > Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email and select a role
4. They'll receive an invitation email valid for 7 days

## Roles and Permissions
| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Owner | Full access, billing control, can delete team |
| Admin | Full access except billing and team deletion |
| Editor | Create/edit/delete projects and files |
| Viewer | Read-only access to shared projects |

## Removing Members
Admins and Owners can remove members from **Settings > Team > Members > [member] > Remove**.
Removed members lose access immediately. Their created content remains in the team.
