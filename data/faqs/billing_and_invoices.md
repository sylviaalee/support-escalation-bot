# Billing and Invoices

## Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions renew on the same day each month (your signup date)
- Invoices are generated automatically and emailed to the billing email on file

## Accessing Invoices
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**
2. Download as PDF or view online
3. Invoices are retained for 7 years

## Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise only, min $5,000)

## Updating Payment Info
Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Payment Methods** to add or change cards.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. We retry after 3, 7, and 14 days
3. After 3 failed attempts, your account is suspended (not deleted)
4. All data is retained for 30 days after suspension
