# Data Privacy and GDPR

## Our Commitments
- We are GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Data is stored in the region you select at signup (US, EU, APAC)
- We never sell your data to third parties

## Data We Collect
- Account information (email, name)
- Usage data (API calls, file activity)
- Payment information (handled by Stripe, we store only last 4 digits)
- IP addresses and device info (for security)

## Your Rights (GDPR)
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data (Settings > Privacy > Export)
- **Rectification**: Update incorrect data in Settings
- **Erasure**: Delete your account removes personal data (see Account Deletion FAQ)
- **Portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Object**: Opt out of analytics under Settings > Privacy

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
Enterprise customers can request a DPA for compliance purposes. Email legal@example.com.

## Subprocessors
Our full list of subprocessors is published at example.com/subprocessors.

## Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted account data: Removed within 30 days (billing records 7 years)
- Backups: Purged within 90 days of deletion
